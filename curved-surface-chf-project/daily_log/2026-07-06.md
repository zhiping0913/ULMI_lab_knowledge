## Scientific conclusions / interpretation

### Normalized 1D gain

For both L and ND/a0 scans, the maximum normalized reflected peak gain

```text
Sx_peak / (a0^2 S_c,M)
```

is roughly `O(10)` and not strongly monotonic in optimized `a0`. This supports a careful statement:

> In optimized ultrathin-foil conditions, medium relativistic `a0` can be comparable to very high `a0` in normalized reflected-peak amplification, but absolute reflected intensity still carries the `a0^2` base.

Physics interpretation: the controlling parameter is closer to areal-density matching / relativistic transparency window (`ND/a0`-like) than to `a0` alone. The local gain is associated with CSE / electron nanobunch radiation in a very short time window.

### Factorization for the next stage

Zhiping emphasized that the next task is to separate:

1. 1D normalized gain / local CSE gain.
2. Spatial focusing gain from curved foil / CHF.
3. Coherence or wavefront quality penalty.

Working factorization:

```text
S_focus^peak/(a0^2 S_c,M) = G_CSE/1D × G_spatial × C_wavefront
```

This should become a central diagnostic roadmap / figure.
